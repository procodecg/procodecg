codeMeetUp()

ProCodeCG codeMeetUp() is a weekly event to gather people to share, to collaborate, to learn, to become mentor, to solve problems together, to expand networking, to help people find talents to be hired or to help jobseeker to find jobs.

We invite speakers from different backgrounds to share about their expertise to public. The topic can be various from art to science, business, psychology, engineering, cinematography and so many more.

This event is also our contribution to the society to share and distribute knowledge, and to create opportunities and enable different kind people to meet each other and make awesome creations.
